Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

CloudEats Ltd ("CloudEats", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it, in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who we are

CloudEats Ltd is a company registered in England and Wales, company number 17308005, registered office: 5 Brayford Square, London, England, E1 0SG. We are the "data controller" for the personal data described in this policy.

2. What information we collect

When you use CloudEats, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Your delivery address, when you place an order
  • Order details, including items ordered and any delivery notes
  • Payment information — processed securely by our payment provider, Stripe. We do not store your full card details ourselves.
  • Basic technical information such as browser type and IP address, for security and site functionality

3. How we use your information

We use your personal data to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders, including passing relevant order details to the kitchen preparing your food and, where applicable, the driver delivering it
  • Process payments securely via Stripe
  • Communicate with you about your order, including confirmations and support enquiries
  • Maintain the security and proper functioning of our platform
  • Comply with our legal obligations, including tax and accounting requirements

4. Who we share your information with

We share your information only where necessary to provide our service:

  • Kitchen partners — to prepare and fulfil your order (name, order details, and delivery address where relevant)
  • Delivery drivers — to deliver your order (name, phone number, delivery address, and order details)
  • Stripe, our payment processor, to securely process payments
  • Service providers who help us operate our platform, such as our hosting provider
  • Where required by law, regulators, or to protect our legal rights

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. How long we keep your information

We retain order and payment records for as long as necessary to comply with our legal and tax obligations (typically at least 6 years for financial records). Other personal data is retained only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

6. Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, in certain circumstances
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent, where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@cloudeats.co.uk. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK's data protection regulator, the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

7. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies necessary for the site to function. We do not currently use tracking or advertising cookies. This policy will be updated if that changes.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact us

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your personal data, please contact us at support@cloudeats.co.uk.
