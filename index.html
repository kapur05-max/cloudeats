Food Delivery with CloudEats

CloudEats is a food delivery platform connecting customers with home kitchens, cloud kitchens, dark kitchens, ghost kitchens, tiffin services, and takeaways across London, Birmingham, and Manchester.

Delivery services will be available as kitchens and drivers join and go live on the platform.

Cloud Kitchen UK

A cloud kitchen UK is a delivery-only kitchen designed for online food orders. CloudEats helps chefs launch cloud kitchens and food brands without opening a traditional restaurant.

Ghost Kitchen UK

Ghost kitchens allow food brands to operate delivery-first restaurants. CloudEats helps chefs run ghost kitchens and reach customers across the UK.

Dark Kitchen UK

Dark kitchens focus entirely on takeaway and food delivery. Many restaurants expand their brands using dark kitchens.

Home Kitchen Business

Starting a home kitchen business allows chefs to cook from home and sell meals online while building their own food brand.
