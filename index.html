Frequently Asked Questions

What is a cloud kitchen?
A cloud kitchen is a delivery-only kitchen that prepares food for online orders without a traditional restaurant dining area.

What is a ghost kitchen?
A ghost kitchen is a commercial kitchen used by food brands that operate mainly through delivery platforms.

What is a dark kitchen?
A dark kitchen is similar to a cloud kitchen and focuses on preparing meals for takeaway and delivery orders.

Can I start a home kitchen business in the UK?
Yes, many chefs start a home kitchen business by registering with their local council and following UK food safety regulations.

Ghost Kitchen UK

A ghost kitchen UK is a professional kitchen where food brands prepare meals for delivery platforms. Many modern food startups operate ghost kitchens to reach customers without dine-in restaurants. CloudEats connects ghost kitchens with customers looking for exciting takeaway food brands.

Dark Kitchen UK

Dark kitchen UK businesses focus entirely on delivery and takeaway food. Restaurants and food entrepreneurs use dark kitchens to expand their brands quickly. CloudEats helps chefs launch dark kitchens and connect with customers across London, Manchester and other UK cities.

Home Kitchen Business
