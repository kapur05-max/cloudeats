Frequently Asked Questions

What is a cloud kitchen?
A cloud kitchen is a delivery-only kitchen that prepares food for online orders without a traditional restaurant dining area.

What is a ghost kitchen?
A ghost kitchen is a commercial kitchen used by food brands that operate mainly through delivery platforms.

What is a dark kitchen?
A dark kitchen is similar to a cloud kitchen and focuses on preparing meals for takeaway and delivery orders.

Can I start a home kitchen business in the UK?
Yes, many chefs start a home kitchen business by registering with their local council and following UK food safety regulations.
