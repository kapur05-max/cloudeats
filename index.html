A cloud kitchen UK is a delivery-only kitchen designed for online food orders. Many chefs launch food brands using cloud kitchens without opening traditional restaurants.

Ghost kitchens allow food brands to operate delivery-first restaurants without dine-in spaces. CloudEats helps chefs grow ghost kitchen brands across the UK.

Dark kitchens are professional kitchens designed only for takeaway and delivery food. Restaurants use dark kitchens to expand their brands quickly.
