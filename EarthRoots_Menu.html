About Cloudzio

Cloudzio is a marketplace connecting customers with independent kitchens across London — from cloud kitchens, home kitchens, independent kitchens, dark kitchens, ghost kitchens, and commercial kitchens. Beyond food delivery, Cloudzio also helps kitchen owners advertise and rent out their space to other food businesses.

We started Cloudzio because we believe great food shouldn't only come from big chains. Every kitchen on our platform is run by real people pouring their passion into what they cook — and every driver plays a part in bringing that food to your door. We're here to help them grow, one order at a time.

Who Cloudzio is for

Customers — discover real, independent food near you

Kitchen owners — reach more customers and keep more of what you earn

Drivers — flexible work, paid per delivery

Kitchen space owners — advertise or rent out your space to other food businesses
